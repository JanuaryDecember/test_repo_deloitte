# Deloitter - hackathon MVP

A Tinder-style app that connects people with similar interests or competencies. The target user is a Deloitte employee who wants to meet people within their organization.

### What the app does

- Matches people in a Tinder-like fashion
- Shows shared interests and competencies
- Recommends people with a common professional background
- Displays matched users and shows a compatibility score for each match
- For POC purposes, includes basic authentication with credentials stored in a PostgreSQL database

### What the app does NOT do

- Does not create a chat between users
- Does not facilitate romantic relationships between employees
- Does not discriminate between possible connections based on competencies or job title

### Success criteria

- A user logs in, swipes through other users just like in Tinder, and is able to match with another employee.
